## System Access

**Login URL**: `http://localhost/vawc-main/login.php`

### How to Login
1. Open the login URL in your browser
2. Enter your 4-digit PIN
3. Click "Login" or press Enter
4. You'll be redirected to the assessment form

---

## Assessment Process

### Step 0: Initial Information
- Assessor name (auto-filled from your PIN)
- Select barangay
- Choose assessment date

### Step 1: Section 1 - Establishment (25 points)
Questions about VAW Desk establishment, training, location, and facilities

### Step 2: Section 2 - Resources (25 points)
Questions about furniture, equipment, monitoring tools, and references

### Step 3: Section 3 - Policies & Plans (25 points)
Questions about AIP, GAD Plan, and BDP integration

### Step 4: Section 4 - Accomplishments (25 points)
Questions about reports, database updates, and SOBA inclusion

### Step 5: Review & Submit
Review all scores and submit the assessment

---

## Scoring System

- **Total Score**: 100 points (divided into 4 sections of 25 points each)
- **Automatic Calculation**: Scores are calculated automatically based on your answers
- **No Manual Entry**: Select checkboxes and radio buttons; scores update in real-time
